If you have lost or found something, tap the + at the top to create a report. Items you have borrowed shall be marked found. In case you can't be easily reached by school email, please include contact details in the description.

Browse reports to find ones of opposite type matching your item. Alternatively, use one of the two buttons above to view a list of automatically generated report pairs. These are also displayed on the details page of a report.

Once you have found a report which you believe describes an item your have lost/found/borrowed, message the report author privately (using the contact details from the description, if provided) and arrange for pickup. When the case is resolved, both sides should delete the corresponding reports on their ends.
